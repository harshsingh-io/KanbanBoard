# Palette's Journal

## 2024-05-22 - Accessibility in Android XML
**Learning:** Android accessibility often requires `android:contentDescription` for ImageViews and `android:hint` combined with `android:inputType` for EditTexts.  `android:autofillHints` is also crucial for forms.
**Action:** When auditing layouts, check for `contentDescription` on non-decorative images and ensure `EditText` fields have proper input types and autofill hints.
