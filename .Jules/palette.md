## 2024-05-24 - [Form Accessibility Patterns]
**Learning:** This Android app's forms consistently missed `android:autofillHints` and sometimes used incorrect `inputType` (e.g., `textEmailAddress` for names), which degrades keyboard usability and password manager integration.
**Action:** Future form improvements must prioritize proper `inputType` and `autofillHints` to ensure smooth data entry and accessibility compliance.
